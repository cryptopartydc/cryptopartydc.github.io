Cryptoparty

Inspired by the global Cryptoparty movement. We believe privacy is a fundamental human right. It is a socially constructed concept that varies between users. We focus on the tools of digital security and leave attendees to define their own best practices.

Code for DC

Cryptoparty is a learning group part of Code for DC, a Code for America Brigade in Washington, DC. We invite all, regardless of technical background, who are interested in learning about and practicing strong digital security.

Empower

Each event is facilitated by a member of our community. Topics are selected based on group interest and are intended to serve both the beginner and veteran cryptographer. Get in touch with us on Slack or via email if you're interested in facilitating a session or have questions.
